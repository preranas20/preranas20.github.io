About Me

I am a Graduate student at the University of North Carolina, Charlotte. I am currently pursuing my master's in Computer Science with specialization in Intelligent and Interactive Systems.

I started my career at Accenture Solutions in 2013 as an Associate Software Engineer. In my 3 years of work experience, I was involved in Java and SAP Process Integration team. I have worked with clients from some of the top tier manufacturers dealing in Automotive and Steel Industry. Working on large scale projects as a developer, I got a chance to envision, design and develop performance-driven scalable software solutions in an agile environment.

My interest in computer science takes me down the memory lane when I created my first web page using HTML. Building on the foundation and curiosity from this experience, I pursued my undergraduate degree in Information Technology. Taking my motivation to a higher level, I am now pursuing my master's in computer science. I am passionate about programming and developing quality software systems for web and mobile platforms. I believe in lifelong learning with an enthusiasm to explore new cutting-edge technologies. I love to take up new challenges and solve complex problems resulting in an efficient solution. When I am not coding, I enjoy cooking, swimming and obsessing over good books to read.

I am always eager to learn and work on challenging projects. In case you have new problem to be solved or great idea to be developed, feel free to connect with me.
